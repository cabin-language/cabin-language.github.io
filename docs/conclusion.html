Additional Support

For additional support on using Cabin and specfic errors you're getting in your Cabin code, check out the community. We're a friendly and accepting bunch, and are happy to help out beginners with their struggles learning the language.

Contributing

If you like Cabin, and you're interested in being a part of it, consider contributing! The Cabin team is in desperate need of a wide variety of creators, including programmers, graphic designers, and more. It's totally non-commital; Feel free to make one cool little thing and leave it at that. Cabin will never use your creations (be it code, designs, etc.) without your explicit permission. You can read more about contributing in the community tab.
